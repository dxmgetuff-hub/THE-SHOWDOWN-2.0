# THE SHOWDOWN 2.0

**Red Team vs Blue Team - Roblox Competitive Gaming Tournament**

## 🎮 Overview
THE SHOWDOWN 2.0 is an exciting competitive gaming tournament hosted on Roblox where players compete in teams to win exclusive prizes!

## 🏆 Tournament Details

### Prize
🎁 **1 Month Discord Nitro** to the winning team

### Team Structure
- 🔴 **Red Team**
- 🔵 **Blue Team**

### How It Works
- Players join either Red Team or Blue Team
- Teams compete in competitive Roblox gameplay
- Tournament organizers will share their screen during matches
- **No Pay-to-Win mechanics** - Fair competition for all players

## 📋 Rules & Conduct Policy

### Player Code of Conduct
✅ **Required:**
- Respect all staff members and players
- Follow all tournament rules
- Maintain sportsmanship throughout competition

❌ **Zero Tolerance:**
- **Violence or threats toward staff** = **IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION**
- Disqualification applies regardless of timing or tournament stage
- No exceptions will be made for late arrivals who violate conduct

### Fair Play
- This is a **NO PAY-TO-WIN** tournament
- All players compete on equal footing
- No unfair advantages allowed
- Equal access to game resources for both teams

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Register** - Join the tournament
2. **Choose Your Team** - Red Team or Blue Team
3. **Prepare** - Practice and coordinate with teammates
4. **Compete** - Participate in tournament matches
5. **Win** - First place team receives 1 Month Discord Nitro!

## 📺 Tournament Format

Tournament organizers will provide screen sharing for:
- Live match commentary
- Fair play verification
- Spectator experience
